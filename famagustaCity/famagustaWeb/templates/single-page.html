Covid-19 Chronic Disease Evaluation Form

57/1979 Within the framework of Article 16 of the Law on the Establishment Principles of Ministries, a temporary Covid -19 Chronic Disease Evaluation Commission has been established.
Personnel working in all Public Institutions and Organizations; Due to the Covid-19 outbreak, if he thinks that his current health situation poses a high risk for working life, he will be able to apply to the Covid-19 Chronic Disease Evaluation Commission.
Applications will be made as follows:
1. The staff will inform their physician about their situation. If the doctor deems necessary as a result of his evaluation, he will fill in the relevant section of the attached COVID-19 Chronic Disease Assessment Form. The staff will apply to the Institution to which they are affiliated with the following documents.
A doctor report documenting the disease, the medications he uses and a prescription showing how long he will use, Tests documenting his disease,
